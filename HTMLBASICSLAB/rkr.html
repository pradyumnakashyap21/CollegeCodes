Oia in Bengaluru is Asia's largest pub, famed for its Santorini-inspired ambiance with whitewashed walls,
blue accents, and tranquil decor, offering a Mediterranean escape with Pan-Asian & Italian food, signature
cocktails, and extensive seating for over 1800 guests, making it a visually stunning but sometimes pricey
destination with a blend of impressive vibes and mixed reviews on service and food consistency.
